I am a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India, where I have worked with Srinivasan Iyengar, Mohit Jain, and Manohar Swaminathan.

Research Interests

I work broadly in the intersection of HCI, sensing, and applied AI/ML. Specifically, I like to build systems that employ sensing, algorithms, AI/ML to explore HCI questions. Application areas of my interest are health, ICTD, education, sustainability, and sports. My work has been published at CSCW, ACM COMPASS (Best Paper Award), and ACM SenSys (poster). Some of my work is also open sourced by Microsoft: github.com/microsoft/modulo.
